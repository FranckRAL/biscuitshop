Our Story

Founded in 2020, Biscuit'tsika started with a simple mission: to bring joy to people's lives through delicious, handcrafted biscuits.

Our founder, Franck Andritina, began baking in her small kitchen, experimenting with unique flavors and recipes that quickly gained popularity among friends and family.

"Today, we are proud to be a beloved local bakery, known for our commitment to quality ingredients and exceptional taste."

Each biscuit is made with love and care, ensuring that every bite is a delightful experience.
